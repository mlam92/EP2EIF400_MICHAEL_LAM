Who are we?

Vision

Leading like school , development of information technologies and communication in Costa Rica through its creation, promotion and use in different areas of knowledge as an engine for human development to help achieve a just, equitable society solidarity

Mission

Training highly competitive professionals in information technology and communication, and educational information , and contribute to the training of the population in the use of information technology to promote human development of Costa Rican society .

Strategic goals

* The training of professionals responsible and committed to the technological and human development.

* Strengthening the technological culture of the society in its various activities.

* Research to improve the application of information technology and communication in society .

* Continuous updating of its staff to improve teaching quality and to meet the needs of Costa Rican society .
